--- Workflow Specification (Textual Description) ---

This workflow covers the process of a student taking quizzes within a
course, receiving grades and feedback, and earning a certificate upon course
completion. As the student progresses through course modules and completes
lectures, they encounter quizzes at the end of each module. The student
starts a quiz, answers all multiple-choice questions, and submits it for
grading. The system automatically evaluates the answers, calculates the
score, and presents the results with a breakdown showing which questions
were correct and which were wrong. If the student achieves the passing
score, the module quiz is marked as passed; otherwise, the student may
reattempt the quiz. Once the student has completed all lectures and passed
all module quizzes in a course, the system recognizes the course as fully
completed. The student can then generate a certificate of completion which
is displayed on screen with their name, course title, completion date, and
a unique certificate ID, and can be downloaded as a PDF.

--- Workflow Description (Step-by-Step) ---

Step 1: Navigating to a Module Quiz
   - The student is progressing through a course and has completed the
     video lectures within a module.
   - At the end of the module, the sidebar displays a "Module Quiz" item.
   - The student clicks on the quiz to open the quiz information page.
   - The quiz information page displays: quiz title, number of questions,
     and the passing score requirement (e.g., 70%).
   - The student clicks "Start Quiz" to begin the attempt.

Step 2: Answering Quiz Questions
   - The system loads the quiz interface displaying all questions on the
     page.
   - For each multiple-choice question, the student selects one answer from
     the provided options by clicking the corresponding radio button.
   - A counter shows how many questions have been answered out of the total
     (e.g., "5 of 10 answered").
   - The student works through all questions at their own pace.

Step 3: Submitting the Quiz
   - After answering all questions, the student clicks "Submit Quiz."
   - A confirmation dialog appears: "Are you sure you want to submit?"
   - The student confirms, and the system accepts the submission for
     grading.

Step 4: Automated Grading & Score Calculation
   - The system compares each submitted answer against the correct answer
     stored in the database.
   - The system calculates: total correct answers, total incorrect answers,
     and the overall score as a percentage.
   - The system determines whether the student has passed or failed based
     on the passing score threshold set by the instructor.

Step 5: Viewing Quiz Results & Feedback
   - The system displays the results page showing: the student's score
     (e.g., "8 out of 10 — 80%") and pass/fail status highlighted in
     green for pass or red for fail.
   - Below the summary, a question-by-question breakdown is shown:
     * Each question is listed with the student's selected answer and the
       correct answer.
     * Correctly answered questions are marked with a green checkmark.
     * Incorrectly answered questions are marked with a red cross, and the
       correct answer is revealed.
   - The student reviews this feedback to understand their mistakes.

Step 6: Reattempting a Failed Quiz
   - If the student did not achieve the passing score, a "Retake Quiz"
     button is displayed on the results page.
   - The student clicks "Retake Quiz" to start a new attempt.
   - The student goes through the same process of answering and submitting.
   - The system stores the latest attempt's score.
   - The student can reattempt the quiz as many times as needed until they
     pass.

Step 7: Course Completion Check
   - As the student passes each module quiz and marks all lectures as
     complete, the system tracks overall course progress.
   - The system checks two conditions for course completion:
     * All video lectures across all modules are marked as complete.
     * All module quizzes have been passed (score >= passing threshold).
   - Once both conditions are met, the system updates the course status
     to "Completed" on the student's dashboard.
   - A congratulatory message is shown: "Congratulations! You have
     completed [Course Title]!"

Step 8: Certificate Generation & Download
   - On the completed course page, a "Get Certificate" button becomes
     available.
   - The student clicks "Get Certificate."
   - The system generates a certificate containing:
     * The student's full name.
     * The course title.
     * The instructor's name.
     * The date of completion.
     * A unique Certificate ID (e.g., CERT-2025-XXXXXX).
   - The certificate is displayed on screen as a styled certificate view.
   - The student clicks "Download PDF" to save the certificate as a PDF
     file to their device.
   - The certificate is also stored in the student's profile under a
     "My Certificates" section, so they can access it again at any time.

--- Key Features & Functionalities ---

   - Module-end quizzes with multiple-choice questions
   - Automated grading with instant score calculation
   - Pass/fail determination based on instructor-set threshold
   - Detailed results with question-by-question feedback
   - Quiz reattempt functionality for failed attempts
   - Automatic course completion detection (lectures + quizzes)
   - Certificate generation with student name, course, date, and unique ID
   - Certificate download as PDF
   - Persistent certificate storage in student profile
